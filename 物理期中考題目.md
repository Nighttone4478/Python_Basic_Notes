# Chapter 21 — Electric Charge（電荷）

## Test 1

問題: Two electrons (e1 and e2) and a proton (p) lie on a straight line, as shown. The directions of the force of e2 on e1, the force of p on e1, and the total force on e1, respectively, are:

答案: D, ←, →, ←

解釋: e2 與 e1 都是電子，同性相斥，所以 e2 對 e1 的力向左；p 是質子，與電子異性相吸，所以 p 對 e1 的力向右。因為 e2 較靠近 e1，斥力較大，所以合力向左。

## Test 2

問題: A conductor is distinguished from an insulator with the same number of atoms by the number of:

答案: C, nearly free electrons

解釋: 導體有較多幾乎自由的電子可以移動，因此能導電；絕緣體的自由電子很少。

## Test 3

問題: A positively charged metal sphere A is brought into contact with an uncharged metal sphere B. As a result:

答案: A, both spheres are positively charged

解釋: 兩個金屬球接觸後電荷會重新分布，原本帶正電的 A 會使 B 也帶正電，因此兩球皆帶正電。

## Test 4

問題: A wire carries a steady current of 2A. The charge that passes a cross section in 2s is:

答案: E, 4C

解釋: 電流定義為 I = q/t，因此 q = It = 2 × 2 = 4 C。

## Test 5

問題: Two particles, X and Y, are 4m apart. X has a charge of 2Q and Y has a charge of Q. The force of X on Y:

答案: E, has the same magnitude as the force of Y on X

解釋: 依牛頓第三定律，兩粒子互相作用力大小相等、方向相反。

# Chapter 22 — Electric Fields（電場）

## Test 1

問題: An electric field is most directly related to:

答案: D, the force acting on a test charge

解釋: 電場定義為 E = F/q0，因此電場最直接對應到測試電荷所受的力。

## Test 2

問題: The units of the electric field are:

答案: D, N/C

解釋: 由 E = F/q 可知，電場單位為 N/C。

## Test 3

問題: The diagram shows the electric field lines due to two charged parallel metal plates. We conclude that:

答案: B, a proton at X would experience the same force if it were placed at Y

解釋: 平行金屬板間電場近似均勻，因此質子在 X 與 Y 所受電力大小相同。

## Test 4

問題: The diagram shows a particle with positive charge Q and a particle with negative charge −Q. The electric field at point P on the perpendicular bisector of the line joining them is:

答案: A, ↑

解釋: 在垂直平分線上，兩電荷造成的水平電場分量互相抵消，垂直分量同方向相加，因此電場向上。

## Test 5

問題: Two charged particles are arranged as shown. In which region could a third particle, with charge +1 C, be placed so that the net electrostatic force on it is zero?

答案: A, I only

解釋: 在 I 區，+1 C 受到 +2 C 的排斥力與 −4 C 的吸引力方向相反，且可能在某點大小相等，所以合力可為零。

# Chapter 23 — Gauss' Law（高斯定律）

## Test 1

問題: A charged point particle is placed at the center of a spherical Gaussian surface. The electric flux ΦE is changed if:

答案: D, the point charge is moved to just outside the sphere

解釋: 高斯定律指出電通量只與封閉面內的淨電荷有關。若點電荷移到球面外，包住的電荷改變，電通量才改變。

## Test 2

問題: A point particle with charge q is at the center of a Gaussian surface in the form of a cube. The electric flux through any one face of the cube is:

答案: D, q / 6ε0

解釋: 總電通量為 q/ε0。立方體有 6 個對稱面，所以每一面的通量為 q/(6ε0)。

## Test 3

問題: Charge is distributed uniformly on the surface of a large flat plate. The electric field 2cm from the plate is 30N/C. The electric field 4cm from the plate is:

答案: C, 30N/C

解釋: 大平板附近的電場近似為均勻電場，與距離無關，因此仍為 30 N/C。

## Test 4

問題: Which of the following graphs represents the magnitude of the electric field as a function of the distance from the center of a solid charged conducting sphere of radius R?

答案: E, E

解釋: 帶電導體球內部電場為 0；球外等效為點電荷，電場大小隨 1/r² 下降。

## Test 5

問題: Positive charge Q is placed on a conducting spherical shell with inner radius R1 and outer radius R2. A point charge q is placed at the center of the cavity. The magnitude of the electric field at a point outside the shell, a distance r from the center, is:

答案: D, (q + Q) / 4πε0r²

解釋: 對殼外的高斯面而言，包住的總電荷為 Q + q，所以 E = (Q + q)/(4πε0r²)。

# Chapter 24 — Electric Potential（電位）

## Test 1

問題: The potential difference between two points is 100V. If a particle with a charge of 2C is transported from one of these points to the other, the magnitude of the work done is:

答案: A, 200 J

解釋: W = qΔV = 2 × 100 = 200 J。

## Test 2

問題: A particle with mass m and charge −q is projected with speed v0 into the region between two parallel plates as shown. The potential difference between the two plates is V and their separation is d. The change in kinetic energy of the particle as it traverses this region is:

答案: C, qV

解釋: 動能變化等於電場做功，大小由電荷量與電位差決定，因此為 qV。

## Test 3

問題: An electron goes from one equipotential surface to another along one of the four paths shown below. Rank the paths according to the work done by the electric field, from least to greatest.

答案: D, 4 and 2 tie, then 3, then 1

解釋: 靜電力為保守力，做功只與起點、終點電位差有關，與路徑形狀無關；依各路徑的電位差排序即可。

## Test 4

問題: Two particles with charges Q and −Q are fixed at the vertices of an equilateral triangle with sides of length a. If k = 1 / 4πε0, the work required to move a particle with charge q from the other vertex to the center of the line joining the fixed particles is:

答案: A, 0

解釋: 由於 +Q 與 −Q 對該點的電位貢獻互相抵消，起點與終點電位差為 0，因此所需功為 0。

## Test 5

問題: A conducting sphere with radius R is charged until the magnitude of the electric field just outside its surface is E. The electric potential of the sphere, relative to the potential far away, is:

答案: D, ER

解釋: 球面外 E = kQ/R²，球面電位 V = kQ/R，因此 V = ER。

# Chapter 25 — Capacitance（電容）

## Test 1

問題: A farad is the same as a:

答案: C, C/V

解釋: 電容定義為 C = Q/V，所以法拉的單位是 C/V。

## Test 2

問題: If both the plate area and the plate separation of a parallel-plate capacitor are doubled, the capacitance is:

答案: C, unchanged

解釋: 平行板電容 C = εA/d。若面積 A 與板距 d 同時加倍，比例不變，所以電容不變。

## Test 3

問題: Pulling the plates of an isolated charged capacitor apart:

答案: B, increases the potential difference

解釋: 孤立電容器的 Q 不變；拉開極板使 d 增加、C 變小，由 V = Q/C 可知電位差增加。

## Test 4

問題: Each of the four capacitors shown is 500 μF. The voltmeter reads 1000V. The magnitude of the charge, in coulombs, on each capacitor plate is:

答案: B, 0.5

解釋: Q = CV = 500 × 10⁻⁶ × 1000 = 0.5 C。

## Test 5

問題: The diagram shows four 6-μF capacitors. The capacitance between points a and b is:

答案: C, 6 μF

解釋: 每組兩個 6 μF 串聯得到 3 μF，兩組再並聯，所以等效電容為 6 μF。

# Chapter 28 — Magnetic Fields（磁場）

## Test 1

問題: An electron moves in the negative x direction, through a uniform magnetic field B in the negative y direction. The magnetic force on the electron is:

答案: E, in the negative z direction

解釋: 先用 F = qv × B 判斷正電荷方向，再因電子帶負電，方向相反，因此為 −z 方向。

## Test 2

問題: A magnetic field CANNOT:

答案: D, change the kinetic energy of a charged particle

解釋: 磁力永遠垂直於速度方向，不對粒子做功，因此不能改變動能，只能改變運動方向。

## Test 3

問題: The diagram shows a straight wire carrying current i in a uniform magnetic field. The magnetic force on the wire is indicated by an arrow but the magnetic field is not shown. Of the following possibilities, the direction of the magnetic field is:

答案: E, out of the page

解釋: 載流導線受力方向由 F = IL × B 決定，用右手定則可得磁場方向為出紙面。

## Test 4

問題: The units of magnetic dipole moment are:

答案: C, ampere · meter²

解釋: 磁偶極矩 μ = IA，電流單位為 A，面積單位為 m²，因此單位是 A·m²。

## Test 5

問題: For a loop of current-carrying wire in a uniform magnetic field the potential energy is a minimum if the magnetic dipole moment of the loop is:

答案: A, in the same direction as the field

解釋: 磁偶極位能 U = −μ·B，當 μ 與 B 同方向時，U 最小。

# Chapter 29 — Magnetic Fields Due to Currents（電流產生的磁場）

## Test 1

問題: Electrons are going around a circle in a counterclockwise direction as shown. At the center of the circle, they produce a magnetic field that is:

答案: A, into the page

解釋: 電子帶負電，等效傳統電流方向與電子運動方向相反；再用右手定則判斷，圓心磁場方向為進入紙面。

## Test 2

問題: A wire carrying a large current i from east to west is placed over an ordinary magnetic compass. The end of the compass needle marked “N” will point:

答案: B, south

解釋: 用右手定則判斷直導線周圍磁場方向，指南針 N 端會沿磁場方向轉動，因此指向南方。

## Test 3

問題: Two long straight current-carrying parallel wires cross the x axis and carry currents I and 3I in the same direction, as shown. At what value of x is the net magnetic field zero?

答案: C, 3

解釋: 長直導線磁場 B = μ0I/(2πR)。兩導線間磁場方向相反，令兩者大小相等，可得零磁場位置在靠近小電流導線處，對應 x = 3。

## Test 4

問題: If R is the distance from a magnetic dipole, then the magnetic field it produces is proportional to:

答案: E, 1/R³

解釋: 磁偶極子的遠場磁場大小與距離三次方成反比，即 B ∝ 1/R³。

## Test 5

答案: B, 3, 2, 1

解釋: 圓弧在圓心產生的磁場與 φ/R 成正比。比較各電路的圓弧角度與半徑後，大小排序為 3, 2, 1。

# Chapter 31 — Electromagnetic Oscillations and Alternating Current（電磁振盪與交流電）

## Test 1

問題: The rapid exponential decay in just a few cycles of the charge on the plates of capacitor in an RLC circuit might be due to:

答案: D, a large resistance

解釋: 電阻會消耗電能，使振盪能量轉成熱能；電阻越大，衰減越快。

問題: In a purely capacitive circuit, the current:

答案: A, leads the voltage by one-quarter cycle

解釋: 純電容電路中，電壓落後電流 90°，等價於電流領先電壓四分之一個週期。

問題: In a purely resistive circuit, the current:

答案: E, is in phase with the voltage

答案: B, decrease
